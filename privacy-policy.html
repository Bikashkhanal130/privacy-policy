Privacy Policy for Social Media Content Filter Chrome Extension

Effective Date: June 14, 2025

Your privacy is very important to us. This Chrome extension, Social Media Content Filter, is designed to operate without collecting personally identifiable information. Below we explain what data we do and do not collect.

Information We Collect

  • We use chrome.storage to save user-defined settings (e.g., blocked keywords and custom filter options) locally in your browser.
  • We may fetch external static configuration files such as keyword blacklists from a secure remote server to update filtering rules dynamically.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, IP address, or passwords.
  • We do not track or monitor your browsing history.
  • We do not share any data with third-party services.

Permissions Explained

  • Host permissions are used to enable content filtering functionality on specific social media sites.
  • Remote code is used only to load static resources like keyword lists. No executable scripts are dynamically loaded or evaluated via eval().

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us at: admin@gadgetzonenepal.com

By using this extension, you agree to this policy. We may update this policy from time to time and will reflect the changes on this page.
